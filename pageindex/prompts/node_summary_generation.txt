**Summary Quality Guidelines:**
- **Length**: Aim for 1-2 sentences (20-50 words), maximum 3 sentences for complex sections

- **Completeness**: Capture the section's purpose and main takeaways
- **Objectivity**: Present factual information without interpretation or opinion
